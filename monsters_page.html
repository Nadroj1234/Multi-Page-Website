The Main Monsters In R.E.P.O

The Robe

The Robe is a mysterious, cloaked entity known for silently stalking the player through dimly lit corridors..

Apex Predator

If touched, it transforms into a deadly beast that rushes and shreds players.

The Spewer

Latches onto players and spews poison, causing area damage and damaging loot.

The Peeper

Grabs players from above and drains health while staring directly at them. You have to break line of sight to escape.

The Shadow Child

Harmless if ignored, but stare at it too long and it will toss you and cause damage.
