The Upgrades

Stamina

  • Cost: $2,000
  • Effect: Adds 10 points to your stamina, enabling longer sprint durations and reducing fatigue.

Health

  • Cost: $6,000–$8,000
  • Effect: Increases maximum health by 20 points, allowing you to withstand more damage.

Sprint Speed

  • Cost: $6,000–$8,000
  • Effect: Increases your sprinting speed, facilitating quicker escapes and faster navigation.

Strength

  • Cost: $6,000–$8,000
  • Effect: Enhances your carrying capacity, allowing you to transport heavier objects without slowing down.

Range

  • Cost: $6,000–$8,000
  • Effect: Extends your grab range, making it easier to pick up items from a distance.

Tumble Launch

  • Cost: $4,000–$5,000
  • Effect: Increases the distance covered when performing a dodge roll, aiding in evasion. ​

Extra Jump

  • Cost: $12,000
  • Effect: Grants an additional mid-air jump, allowing you to reach higher areas or evade threats more effectively. ​

Map Player Count

  • Cost: $8,000–$12,000
  • Effect: Displays the number of active players on your map, helping you track teammates' locations and statuses. ​
